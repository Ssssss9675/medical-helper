# medical-helper # Medical Helper

**Medical Helper** is a simple mobile app that helps ordinary people understand their medical reports.  
Upload a photo of a blood test, X‑ray, MRI, or CT scan – the app will read the text and explain it in plain, easy‑to‑understand language. It also suggests what steps to take next, like talking to a doctor.

## Features (in development)
- Google Sign‑in
- Upload medical report images (PDF or photo)
- AI‑powered text extraction
- Simple explanation with a clear medical disclaimer

## Purpose
Medical reports are often written at a high reading level, leaving patients confused and worried.  
This app aims to bridge that gap by providing a free, easy‑to‑use tool that translates medical jargon into everyday language.

## Disclaimer
This app is for educational purposes only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for any medical concerns.
